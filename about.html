About Us

As a local trade organization, AGC Houston has been representing the best interests of the commercial construction industry of Greater Houston for more than 95 years. While a representative of the industry at large, the association is supported by a membership of over 550 firms. This includes general and specialty contractors, as well as service and supply companies. These firms employ over 27,000 individuals in the region.

Government Affairs & Advocacy

AGC Houston works to promote and protect our industry's interests and practices through our Government Affairs Committee (GAC). Working in tandem with advocacy groups and the AGC Texas Building Branch in Austin, the GAC ensures that legislators and regulators understand how policies impact the construction industry.

The GAC addresses industry issues with city and county governments as well as the Port of Houston, school districts and other governmental entities. Through our advocacy efforts and PAC contributions, we provide an impactful and respected voice in the policy-making process. Contact William Martinez for details on how you can serve on this committee.
