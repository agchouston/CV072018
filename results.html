2018 General Election Results

Key Races in the AGC Houston Region

Includes select races financially supported by the AGC of America PAC, AGC Texas Building Branch PAC and AGC Houston PAC.


Statewide: Republicans won all offices on the ballot by the smallest margins we've seen in 20 years.

Texas Courts of Appeals: Texas Courts of Appeals in Austin, Dallas, San Antonio, Houston and Corpus Christi have been completely swept by Democrats.

Texas Senate: Democrats picked up two seats: Burton and Huffines. The partisan split will move from 21 Rs/10 Ds to 19 Rs/12 Ds. One seat will soon be vacated by Sen. Sylvia Garcia, who won her bid for CD 29. The Governor must call a special election to fill that seat.

Texas House: Democrats picked up 12 seats with several Republicans barely winning. The partisan split will move from 95 Rs/55 Ds to 83 Rs/67 Ds.
